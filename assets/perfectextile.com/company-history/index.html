Why are we called Pengfei, also known as perfectextile

Pengfei is a family owned enterprise with two factories, an import and export company – Zhuoyi, and a perfectextile brand

My Childhood

In 2004, when I was just a child, my parents laid the foundation of what would eventually become a lifelong passion for me. They established Pengfei Textile Factory, a modest operation that produced greige fabric . I grew up surrounded by textiles, watching my parents navigate the intricate world of fabric production. Little did I know, these early years would shape my future.

At that time, the factory was small, but it carried our family’s hopes and dreams for the future. Today, after twenty years of development, our company has accumulated extensive experience and knowledge in the industry, establishing a complete supply chain from design to manufacturing and sales. To meet the ever-changing market demands, we have continuously upgraded our factory layout and equipment, introducing the latest production technologies to ensure our products meet international standards. However, despite the modern facilities and scale we now possess, we have chosen to preserve the very first factory built over 20 years ago. This factory no longer serves as our primary production site, but it remains the place where our dreams first took flight. Every time I pass by it, I am reminded of my parents' early days, working tirelessly with unwavering determination. It represents the struggles and the commitment they had at the start of their entrepreneurial journey. Keeping this factory is a constant reminder for us — to always stay true to our original purpose. It is this spirit of staying true to our roots that has driven us forward in a highly competitive market.So our brand was born,perfectextile. Which the pronouncation is the same as my parents’ first enterprise factory “ pengfei “ whenever how we grow and how big market stage we are ,We always adhere to the principle of 'never forget the original intention, where dreams begin' and carry our parents' dreams. We aim to become a better company, produce more high-quality products, and take our parents' initial dreams to a bigger stage.striving to bring the warmth and comfort of home to every customer. Looking ahead, we will continue on this path, holding firm to our principles, innovating constantly, and delivering even higher quality products and services to our customers.

During the study abroad period

Fast forward to 2010, when I moved to the UK for my studies. It was there that I truly discovered the beauty and complexity of the textile industry. Studying abroad broadened my horizons, allowing me to understand not just the technical aspects of fabric making, but also the cultural and artistic influences that make textiles so unique. This experience ignited a deeper passion in me, and I knew then that textiles were my calling.

Returning to China to start a business

In 2012, I returned to China with a clear vision and a desire to expand on my family’s legacy. I founded Zhuoyi Trading Company, determined to bring the high-quality fabrics produced by our factory to the global stage. Over the next few years, we built strong relationships with clients worldwide, proving that Chinese fabrics could compete at the highest level.

By 2018, I knew it was time to take the next step. I established Perfectextile, a brand that not only offers premium fabrics but also embodies our commitment to quality and innovation. The launch of our website allowed us to reach a broader audience, offering a seamless experience to customers around the globe. Perfect Textile became the culmination of my journey, from childhood curiosity to a global brand.

our mission

At Perfectextile, our mission is to put our customers at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to providing exceptional bedding and polyester fabric solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and comfort. With over 20 years of industry experience, we continuously innovate and refine our processes to deliver products that fulfill the specific needs of our clients. By managing our supply chain efficiently and maintaining strict quality control, we ensure reliability and satisfaction with every order.
