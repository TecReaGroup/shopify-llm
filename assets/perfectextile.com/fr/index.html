Company History

Pengfei was founded by my parents in 2004. At that time, our factory was only sold in China. Later, I went to study abroad in the UK and gradually realized that this type of product was also needed abroad. In 2012, the factory was gradually handed over to me, and I founded Perfecttextile Company to sell Chinese textiles overseas.
